Take the following steps to request account deletion on Carloaded.com

Request Account Deletion on Carloaded.com

To request account deletion on Carloaded.com, Take the following steps:

  1. Log in to your Carloaded.com account
  2. Click on your profile picture at the top right corner of the page
  3. Click on Settings
  4. Click on Account
  5. Click on Delete Account
  6. Click on Delete Account again to confirm

Note: Deleting your account will remove all your listings and information from Carloaded.com. You will not be able to recover your account once it is deleted.
The following information will be removed from Carloaded.com:

  1. All your profile information including your name, email, phone number and password
  2. Your listings
  3. Your messages
  4. Your account settings

Need help to delete your account? Please send an email to support@@carloaded.com
